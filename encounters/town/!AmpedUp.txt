There is a man who is unable to speak and can only make grunts.
You can attempt to understand him with either animal handling or speak with 
animals. DC 15

Upon success you learn hes telling you about a magic power he made that amps you up.
You can follow him and he will give you some. Taking the powder will remove your 
ability to speak for the next 24 hours.

People who are unable to speak can also be hurt and killed.

Upon telling the guards about the drug den you'll notice there's a 50 percent chance a guard 
can only speak in grunts and a 1/10 chance anyone else but shop keeps can. The king from there 
on out will be unable to speak.
